Beef Stroganoff

Description

This is one of my personal favorites: my mom's beef stroganoff. If you have a picky eaters in your family, you can be sure that even they will love this recipe. You can supplement with noodles, rice, or anything else you choose.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of stew beef
  • 1/2 can beef broth
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 small sweet onion-chopped
  • 1 container of mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 package noodles of choice

Steps

  1. Sautee onions with olive oil until soft.
  2. Put the onions and stew beef in into a deep stew pot on medium heat. After a few minutes, stir. Wait until the beef is fully browned.
  3. Add the beef broth, cream of mushroom soup, and the mushrooms. Bring pot to a simmer.
  4. Simmer for 2 hours, occanionally stir.
  5. Cook noodles, add to pot.
